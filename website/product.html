Q-LOCK Technology

Q-LOCK applies a mathematically constrained perturbation operator to quantum circuits, embedding a latent identity signature while preserving functional equivalence.

Core Capabilities

  • Identity-locked latent vectors (SHA-256 based)
  • Deterministic micro-rotation perturbations
  • Noise-consistent fidelity behavior across backends
  • Pulse-ready annotations for future scheduling layers

Supported Circuit Types

  • OPENQASM 2.0 (native)
  • OPENQASM 3.0 (annotation only)
  • Qiskit QuantumCircuit objects
